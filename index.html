About Me

I am a fourth year graduate student at Syracuse University, New York, USA. I am interested in theoretical and computational physics specifically high energy theory, condensed matter theory and qubit device theory. My research focuses on Lattice Field Theory, Tensor Networks, Quantum Computation, Qubit Device Theory, High Performance Computing, Machine Learning and Open Source Software Development.

Research

  • Tensor Networks - Tensor Network formulation of lattice gauge theories and discrete spin models.
  • Quantum Gravity - Discrete quantum gravity and backreaction of matter fields using Dynamical Triangulations.
  • Lattice QCD - Hadron spectroscopy analysis.
  • Anomalies - Symmetric Mass Generation studies using lattice field theory formalism.
  • Quantum Computation - Quantum simulations of lattice gauge theories and discrete spin models.
  • Quantum Device Theory - Theoretical modelling for industrial and research based multi-qubit quantum chips.
